## PAN's Differentiators
- Computer control via voice (terminal, files, apps)
- Active agent that executes commands, not just records
- Persistent intelligence across phone + PC + wearable
- Not locked to any corporation
- Open/self-hosted

## Risks
- Big tech consolidating (Meta bought Limitless, Amazon bought Bee)
- Privacy backlash for always-listening devices
- Hardware manufacturing is hard
